Game overview

Neon necromancy meets tactical survival

Deadwave keeps every match fresh with playlists that flow from new games of Team Deathmatch and Capture the Flag to stylized zombie survival built inside Unreal Engine 5.

  • Fast, kinetic gunplay tuned for pads + mouse.
  • Shared inventory tech between PvP and PvE playlists.
  • Signal-driven narrative arcs that unlock seasonal rituals.
  • Eight stylized launch maps, with more Unreal Engine 5 arenas coming after release.

Core systems

Signal-bent mechanics built for replay.

Signal Arsenal

Advanced weapon sandbox w/ reactive audio, camera shake tuning, and modular attachments tuned for PvP + zombies.

Signature kits include the Pulse Rifle, Rail Cleaver, and Resonant Shotgun.

Cross-Platform Netcode

Epic Online Services stack for matchmaking, lobbies, friends, and future cross-save.

Supports private + public playlists.

Glitch Narrative

Collect encrypted broadcasts mid-match to unlock lore drops, skins, and alt endings.

Seasonal rituals reveal how the new zombie outbreak ripples across stylized ruins.

Dynamic Difficulty

Adaptive director modulates spawn rates, boss buffs, and reward multipliers.

Great for squads of varying skill levels.

Progression and XP

Player progression system and XP that unlocks new maps, characters, and stylized cosmetics.

Seasonal ranks keep veterans pushing deeper into the zombie meta.

Zombie Drops

Zombie drops shower squads with Nukes, Air-Strikes, Double Points, and temporary invincibility moments.

Momentum swings ensure each zombies game feels like a new broadcast.
